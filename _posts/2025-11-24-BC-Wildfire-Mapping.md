This notebook demonstrates wildfire mapping techniques using Python and geospatial data from Google Earth Engine. We explore how to access and explore geospatial data using data from the Sentinel-2 and GOES-16/17 satellites to gain real-time insights on wildfire perimeters in the region.

**Topics covered:**
- Loading and processing satellite data from Google Earth Engine
- Geospatial visualization with Folium
- Interactive mapping techniques
